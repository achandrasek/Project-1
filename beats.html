How To Make a Song

The 4 Big Steps

  1. Create or download a beat in the genre you want to make
  2. Write you're lyrics, include a catchy chorus and verses that tell a story
  3. Record your vocals over the beat in software like Garageband or Logic Pro X
  4. Mix your track, add effects, master, and you're done!
